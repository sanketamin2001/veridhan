TradeCopierPro for NinjaTrader

TradeCopierPro is a NinjaTrader indicator that mirrors trading activity from a single master account to up to 20 follower accounts in real time.

  • Duplicates entries, limit orders, stop losses, take profits, modifications, and cancellations so every linked account stays perfectly synchronized.
  • Ideal for prop firm allocations, investor accounts, and running multiple strategies or risk profiles from a central master.
  • Designed to work alongside strategies such as the Opening Range Bot to simplify multi-account execution and scaling.

TradeCopierPro is intended for traders and firms who need reliable multi-account replication without manual intervention or operational overhead.
